Foundation of PDTA

Prahaar Defence Training Academy,Pune was established by Lt.Col(RETD) Suresh Narayanrao Vaze (PVSM,AVSM,SM) in 2000 just after he returend from the Kargil War. Lt. Col Vaze was always very keen on developing leading gentlemen from boys just out of school. Prahaar DTA is the most elite written exam and SSB training Center. At PDTA we, with the help of our expert faculty and experienced personnel from the SSB train young boys and make them the most noticed candidates at the written as well as the SSBs.
